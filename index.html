Serving Pratap Nagar Since 2005 🏪

Modi Store is a family-owned grocery shop located in the heart of Pratap Nagar, Jaipur. Founded by Mr. Uma Shankar Modi in 2005, our store has been serving the local community with fresh, high-quality groceries at honest prices for over 21 years.

मोदी स्टोर में आपको मिलती है सबसे ताज़ी किराना, सही वज़न और उचित दाम। हम मानते हैं कि हर परिवार को गुणवत्तापूर्ण सामान आसानी से मिलना चाहिए।

We stock over 500+ products including staples, spices, snacks, personal care and household items from trusted brands like Aashirvaad, MDH, Haldiram, Amul, Fortune and more.
